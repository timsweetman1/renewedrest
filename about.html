About Emily

Passionate about empowering parents.

I'm Emily, a certified IPSP® sleep consultant and proud mom of five. I know the joy — and exhaustion — that comes with welcoming a baby into the family, and I've been in the trenches of sleepless nights, desperate Googling, and endless trial-and-error routines.

At Renewed Rest, I'm passionate about empowering parents to create peaceful, predictable routines that work. I believe every child is unique, and so are their sleep needs — which is why I focus on personalized, compassionate solutions that bring rest to the whole family.

When I'm not helping parents reclaim their nights, you'll find me enjoying time with my own crazy crew, sipping my favorite pomegranate cranberry juice, or enjoying some down time when I can!

How I Got Started · 2021

Everything began with
baby number four.

My journey into sleep consulting began with my own challenges as a mom. At the time, I had three little ones and had just welcomed baby number four. What had worked with my other kids simply didn't work this time — and I was at a complete loss.

My daughter wouldn't sleep in long stretches unless she was in a car seat or in my arms, and I was utterly exhausted. I loved her deeply and cherished the snuggles, but I also had three other children who needed me. Our days felt unpredictable and exhausting because her sleep schedule was all over the place — or nonexistent.

Then, a friend told me about her amazing experience with a sleep consultant (which I hadn't even known was a thing!). I decided to give it a try. Not only did she help solve my daughter's sleep struggles, but she changed our lives. By 3 months old, my daughter was sleeping through the night and taking consistent, restorative naps — while still being breastfed and getting all the connection she needed.

For the first time in eight years, I was a mama who was rested. I had the energy to show up for my family, not just survive the day. I was so amazed, I began telling anyone who would listen about the power of sleep consulting.

Fast forward a bit — after lots of research, becoming IPSP® certified, and helping friends and family — I knew this was my calling. Everyone deserves the gift of good sleep. Exhaustion shouldn't be a rite of passage or a badge parents feel forced to wear. Real rest is possible for every family — and I've made it my mission to normalize well-rested parents and children.

Our Mission

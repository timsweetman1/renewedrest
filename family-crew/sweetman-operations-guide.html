Zone Map

Our home runs on three zones. Every crew member is responsible for their zone being guest-ready — clean, stocked, and done with excellence. Zones rotate weekly for Grace, Addie, and Winnie.

Zone 1
Back of House
Kitchen · Bedrooms · Hallway
  • Vacuum & mop kitchen floors
  • Wipe all countertops
  • Unload & reload dishwasher
  • Empty kitchen trash & reline
  • Refill pet water bowl
  • Wipe coffee table
  • Check all beds
  • Vacuum & mop hallway floors & declutter
  • Clean up games
  • Push in all clothes drawers
Zone 2
Front of House
Living Room · Playroom
  • Vacuum & mop living room floors
  • Vacuum rug
  • Declutter & wipe side tables
  • Fold all blankets neatly
  • Declutter whole room
  • Throw away any trash
  • Put away instruments
  • Declutter playroom
  • Restock diapers & wipes
  • Declutter couches
  • Vacuum & mop playroom floors
Zone 3
Guest Experience
Dining · Bathrooms · Entryways
  • Vacuum & mop entryway floors
  • Vacuum entry rugs
  • Declutter both entryways
  • Vacuum & mop dining room floors
  • Push in all dining chairs & wipe table
  • Declutter desk surface
  • Refill pet food & water
  • Empty both bathroom trash cans
  • Wipe sink areas & counters
  • Vacuum & mop bathroom floors
  • Restock toilet paper
★ The second mile: Your zone is done when it's not just clean — it's guest-ready. Ask yourself: "Would I be proud to show this room to someone visiting for the first time?" If yes, you're done. If not, find the one thing that would make it excellent.

Core Values

"Every task done with excellence,
a smile, and a servant's heart."
The Sweetman Family Motto
🤝 Serve others first
Do your zone so that someone else's day is a little easier. Notice what needs doing and do it without being asked. Our home should feel welcoming to everyone who walks through the door.
🏆 Do it with excellence
Not just done — done well. Wipe the whole counter, not just the middle. Make the bed so it looks great. Excellence means you'd be proud to show your zone to a guest.
😊 Happy spirit
How you do something matters as much as what you do. We don't grumble, sigh, or drag our feet. "My pleasure" is our family's response — and we mean it.
🚀 Go the extra mile
Every zone has a second-mile task — a small thing that shows you care beyond the minimum. Look for what wasn't asked and do it anyway. That's what sets our family apart.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Team before self
Finish your zone, then ask "Who can I help?" Older crew members look out for younger ones. We win as a family or we don't win at all.
🔄 Learn every station
Zones rotate so every crew member learns every job. No one is above any task. Knowing how to do it all makes you a stronger person and a better teammate.
★ Crew Recognition
1
Shout out a crew member at dinner who went the extra mile today
2
"Crew Member of the Week" board — anyone can nominate anyone
3
Zone excellence award when all three zones pass the guest-ready test
4
Family celebration when every crew member hits their second-mile task
The Sweetman Crew Pledge
"I will serve my family with a cheerful heart, do my zone with excellence, go the extra mile, and always — always — say 'my pleasure.'"
